## Master papers:

In [this](https://www.medrxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.06.13.20130468v4.full.pdf) paper, [Isaco Meilijson](http://www.math.tau.ac.il/~isaco/) and I used the Random Time Transformation (RTT) method, to convert the horizontal time (time between events) to a vertical time (time until an event occurs) to model the progress patterns of COVID19 in selected countries.

The long debated question of the sex effect on the human brain structure is examined in [this paper](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/biorxiv/early/2020/11/10/2020.11.09.373258.full.pdf) by [Daphna Joel](https://people.socsci.tau.ac.il/mu/daphnajoel/), [Isaco Meilijson](http://www.math.tau.ac.il/~isaco/) and myself. We found that the majority of brain features do not comply with the 'pure-type' hypothesis. Moreover, we found that men and women brains are actually quite similar in structure.
